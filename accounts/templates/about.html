Our Mission

At EcoStat, we aim to empower communities with data-driven insights to improve sustainability. Our AI-powered platform provides sustainability ratings for residential societies, institutions, and communities based on key environmental factors.

What We Do

  • ✅ Energy Usage – Monitoring electricity consumption and renewable energy adoption.
  • ✅ Pollution Levels – Assessing air and water quality for a healthier environment.
  • ✅ Recycling & Waste Management – Tracking efforts in waste segregation and recycling.
  • ✅ Green Cover – Counting trees and green spaces in an area.
  • ✅ Water Conservation – Evaluating rainwater collection and water-saving initiatives.

Why Choose EcoStat?

  • 🌱 Data-Driven Sustainability – Real-time insights for better decision-making.
  • ⚡ AI-Powered Ratings – Advanced algorithms to measure and improve eco-performance.
  • 🤝 Community Engagement – Encouraging societies to take sustainable actions.
  • 🌍 Global Impact – Helping create smarter and greener cities worldwide.

Join Us in Building a Sustainable Future!

EcoStat is more than just a rating system – it's a movement toward environmental responsibility. Let’s work together to make our communities more sustainable!
